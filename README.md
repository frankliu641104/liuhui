In loving memory of my brother.
This page is a small place to keep his story alive — the laughter he brought into every room, the stubborn courage he carried through every challenge, and the quiet ways he shaped the people around him.
He wasn’t perfect, none of us are, but he was real: kind when it mattered, loyal without needing to say it, and always ready to help someone who needed a hand.
I built this space to remember him not by the day he left, but by the years he lived — the moments, the memories, and the impact that still echoes in the people who loved him.
If you’re here, thank you for remembering him with me.
